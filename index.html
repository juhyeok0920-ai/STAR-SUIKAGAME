🌌 STAR SUIKA GAME - Official Guide & Play Tutorial

Welcome to Star Suika Game, a space-themed physics puzzle game powered by the Matter.js engine. Combine cosmic elements, discover stars, and evolve your own miniature universe in this highly addictive browser game!

🎮 How to Play

  • Aim and Drop: Click on your screen or tap on mobile to drop the cosmic element at your desired horizontal location.
  • Merge and Evolve: When two elements of the exact same type collide, they will automatically merge and evolve into a larger, next-level celestial body.
  • Game Over: Be careful not to let the floating elements cross the warning line at the top of the container. Keep your screen clean to score higher!
